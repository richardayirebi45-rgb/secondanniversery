OUR HISTORY

❤️ Our Story

Every beautiful love story has a beginning,
and ours started in the most unexpected way.

We first met during our pre-classes. One day, Ann walked up to me with a smile and asked, "Are you Nana Richie?" I replied, "Yes, I am." She told me she already knew about me because a friend had spoken to her about me. That simple conversation became the start of something truly special.

At first, we were just friends. We spent time together in class, and whenever I forgot my pencils or books, she was always there to help me. As time went on, we grew closer. I loved buying her little treats, especially Blue Skies, because it was her favorite, and seeing her smile made every moment worthwhile.

Soon, walking with her became one of my favorite parts of the day. I would escort her after classes, and every conversation, every laugh, and every smile brought us closer together.

Then came the moment that changed everything.

On 30th August at exactly5:50 PM, our friendship turned into something much deeper. I finally told her how I felt, and to my surprise, she smiled and admitted that she liked me too. For a moment, I was speechless. I paused, and she thought I didn't feel the same, so she started to walk away. I called her back, we talked, cleared the misunderstanding, and from that day, our love story truly began.

Our first date was on 27th December at the beach. It wasn't about where we were—it was about being together. It became one of the memories I'll always treasure.

Like every relationship, ours wasn't perfect. Distance, misunderstandings, and broken trust tested us in ways we never expected. We experienced pain, separation, and moments when it felt like everything was falling apart. Even during those difficult times, the feelings we had for each other never completely disappeared.

Through every challenge, every lesson, and every moment of growth, we found our way back to each other. Today, we are still here stronger, wiser, and holding on with hope, love, and the belief that our story is still being written.

This is only the beginning of our forever. Happy Anniversary, Ann. ❤️
